VR Parachute Simulation

Immersive Virtual Reality Skydiving Experience

Project Overview

VR Parachute Simulation is an immersive virtual reality experience that puts players in the shoes of a skydiver. Built with Unity and optimized for VR platforms, this project delivers realistic physics simulation and stunning visual effects to create an authentic parachuting experience.

The game features accurate wind dynamics, emergency scenarios, and multiple jump locations. Players must master parachute control, landing techniques, and emergency procedures while enjoying breathtaking views from thousands of feet in the air.
