Game Story

Tuana is displaced from her family by an avalanche. Asena, the mother of all Turks and moon god comes to her aid while she falls asleep thinking she can't find her way.

In her journey she will meet with different people who will help her. Players need to manage their 4 resources: Health, Energy, Food and Grass. Their choices through the game reduce them on some point, but players can avoid this with using various items.
