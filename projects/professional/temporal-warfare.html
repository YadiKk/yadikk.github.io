Game Overview

The game places players in a futuristic setting where they control time while taking on dangerous terrorists in intense FPS combat. Using advanced weapons and tactical abilities, players must eliminate threats, manipulate time to gain an advantage, and survive against overwhelming odds. As they progress, they can collect resources to upgrade their gear and unlock new abilities.
