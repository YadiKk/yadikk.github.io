Epic of Gorgud

Azerbaijani Folklore Adventure Game

Project Overview

Epic of Gorgud is an experimental adventure game inspired by the rich folklore and mythology of Azerbaijan. Based on the legendary "Kitabi Dede Gorgud" (Book of Dede Korkut), this project brings ancient tales to life through modern game development techniques.

Players embark on an epic journey through mystical landscapes, encountering legendary heroes, mythical creatures, and ancient wisdom. The game features traditional Azerbaijani music, authentic cultural elements, and immersive storytelling that honors the heritage of the region.

Technical Implementation

Built with Unity and C#, Epic of Gorgud features custom dialogue systems, cultural asset integration, and immersive audio-visual experiences. The project demonstrates the potential of games as cultural preservation tools.

Special attention was given to historical accuracy, cultural sensitivity, and educational value. The game serves as both entertainment and a bridge between ancient traditions and modern technology.
