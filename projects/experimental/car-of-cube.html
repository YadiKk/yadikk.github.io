Game Overview

A unique 3D racing game where players control a cube-shaped vehicle through challenging courses. The objective is simple yet engaging: reach the end of the road while navigating obstacles and maintaining control.

Hint: Don't rush to reach the end of the road - patience and precision are key to success!
